Common Challenges Survivors Face

Trauma can manifest differently in each person, but many survivors share difficult experiences like:

  • Flashbacks or distressing dreams
  • Avoiding people or places that trigger memories
  • Hypervigilance and difficulties trusting others
  • Emotional numbness, shame, or guilt

Recognising these patterns is the first step toward releasing their hold on your everyday life.
