Trauma Therapy

Overcome the lingering effects of traumatic experiences and rediscover emotional well-being.

Why Trauma Therapy?

Traumatic events can leave lasting emotional scars, manifesting as flashbacks, anxiety, depression, or relationship struggles. Our trauma-focused sessions provide a safe, empathetic space to process these experiences, reduce triggers, and reclaim your sense of self.

Specialised Areas of Support

  • Domestic abuse counselling
  • PTSD and complex trauma
  • Childhood or generational trauma
Begin Your Healing

Our Approach

At Talk with Sian, trauma therapy is shaped by person-centred methods and evidence-based tools like EFT Tapping or CBT strategies (if appropriate). Each session is tailored to your comfort level, respecting your boundaries and pace of healing. We collaborate to build coping mechanisms, enhance self-compassion, and gradually desensitise traumatic triggers.

Remote sessions mean you can access professional therapy without leaving the security of your home—vital when dealing with intense anxiety or trust issues.
