Person Centred Therapy in Chester

Experience a genuine therapeutic relationship built on empathy, understanding, and respect. Available online and in person in Penyffordd, Flintshire.

What Is Person Centred Therapy?

Person Centred Therapy is a humanistic approach that empowers you to tap into your own potential for self-discovery and personal growth. At Talk with Sian, we create a safe, non-judgemental environment where you can explore your feelings and experiences—fostering genuine self-awareness and lasting change for individuals across Chester & Flintshire.

Person Centred Therapy is particularly effective if you seek a collaborative, empathetic space to address emotional struggles and develop a deeper understanding of yourself.

Why Choose Person Centred Therapy?

Unlike directive approaches, Person Centred Therapy respects your autonomy and innate potential. Rather than defining a rigid plan, my role is to support you in identifying your goals and facilitating your own insights and breakthroughs.

Key advantages for women:
• Deep sense of acceptance and understanding
• Empowering focus on self-discovery
• Flexible pace aligned with your needs

How Sessions Work

In Person Centred Therapy, I offer empathy, congruence, and unconditional positive regard while you share your experiences. This supportive atmosphere helps you tap into your own resources for insight and growth, whether you're dealing with anxiety, low mood, or simply striving to better understand yourself.

You set the pace and the agenda. Together, we’ll explore whatever feels most pressing or meaningful to you, nurturing a richer sense of self-worth and resilience.

Convenience of Remote Sessions

My Person Centred Therapy sessions are offered remotely, so you can attend from the comfort of your home. This is ideal if you have a busy schedule or prefer a private environment. I use secure video platforms to protect your confidentiality, ensuring you can focus on personal exploration and healing.

Remote therapy means no more geographical barriers—if you’re in Chester, Flintshire, or beyond, Person Centred Therapy support is just a click away.
