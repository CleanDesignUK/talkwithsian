Talk with Sian

Our mission is to provide compassionate and personalised therapy to help you achieve your best self. We combine evidence-based practices with a heart-centred approach to support your mental health journey.

My Story

At Talk with Sian, we believe in the power of therapy to transform lives. Our practice was founded with the vision of creating a safe, supportive space where individuals can explore their thoughts, emotions, and behaviours without judgement. We are committed to providing high-quality care that is tailored to the unique needs of each client.

Sian's approach to therapy is deeply rooted in empathy and understanding. She believes in the importance of creating a collaborative environment where clients feel heard and supported. Her journey into therapy began with a desire to help others navigate their mental health challenges and achieve a sense of balance and fulfilment in their lives.

Contact Us Today

Sian Jones, Licensed Therapist

With over 10 years of experience in the field of mental health, Sian specialises in cognitive-behavioural therapy and mindfulness techniques. She is dedicated to helping her clients achieve a balanced and fulfilling life.
