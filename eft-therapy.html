What Is EFT Tapping?

Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT) is a method that blends talk therapy principles with acupressure. While focusing on a challenging thought or emotion, you gently tap on specific points—often on your face and upper body. This sends calming signals to the brain and can quickly reduce emotional distress.

By working with both mind and body, EFT helps you release tension and restore balance to your energy system. Many women in Chester & Cheshire have reported feeling immediate relief, noticing shifts in anxiety or emotional overwhelm in just a few sessions.

How EFT Helps

EFT has been shown to ease stress and anxiety by tapping away heightened responses and allowing your body to re-learn calm patterns.

Overcome self-doubt or past traumas to experience greater confidence and self-empowerment in everyday life.

Find emotional balance and clarity by releasing blocked feelings that may contribute to stress, sleeplessness, or negative self-talk.

Your EFT Session

Each session is customized for you. We begin by identifying the core issue or emotion you’d like to address, then guide you through tapping sequences. There’s no rigid formula—your personal comfort and progress shape the experience.

A typical session lasts about 50 minutes. Whether you choose an in-person appointment in Chester or a virtual session, you’ll receive the same level of empathic, knowledgeable support.
