Our Remote Services

Welcome to Talk with Sian, where we provide specialised remote counselling services tailored for women in Chester and Cheshire. Our compassionate, expert care helps you navigate life’s challenges with confidence.

All services are provided remotely, ensuring that you can receive support from the comfort of your own home.

Walk-and-Talk Therapy

Experience therapy in the calming embrace of nature. Our walk-and-talk sessions provide a relaxed setting to discuss your concerns, helping you feel more at ease.

Person-Centred Counselling

Our person-centred approach places you at the heart of therapy. We work collaboratively to understand your unique experiences and foster personal growth.

Emotional Freedom Technique (EFT)

EFT combines cognitive therapy with psychological acupuncture to reduce emotional distress. Ideal for managing anxiety, trauma, and even physical pain.

Trauma Therapy

Our trauma therapy is specifically designed to support women through recovery, providing a safe space to heal from past experiences.

Anxiety & Depression Counselling

We offer specialised counselling for managing anxiety and depression, tailored to the unique experiences faced by women, promoting resilience and well-being.

Stress Management

Learn effective strategies to manage stress. Our sessions focus on building resilience, teaching coping mechanisms, and helping you regain balance in your life.
